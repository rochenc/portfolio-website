About Me

Learn more about my journey and approach to UX design

My Story

I'm a passionate UX designer with 8 years of experience creating user-centered digital experiences. I believe in the power of design to solve real problems and make technology more accessible to end users. Aside from building digital experiences, I have designed and created physical products including furniture and consumer products.

My Approach

My approach to UX design always starts with understanding the user, the business, and the domain context to identify strategic opportunities. I believe great design is created through feedback and iterative learning. I enjoy thinking outside the box to solve old problems in new ways. Currently, I'm interested in learning about quantifying and measuring UX impact.
