Robo Consulting

AI-Powered Business Consulting Platform

Concept design for enterprise consulting transformation

Lead Product Designer

Project Overview

Role: Lead Product Designer

Focus: Product vision and concept design

Scope: End-to-end consulting workflow platform

The Vision

Reimagining enterprise consulting workflows through AI-powered automation. Creating a platform where consultants can leverage intelligent agents to enhance their capabilities and deliver better insights to clients.

The Opportunity

The consulting industry faces critical challenges:

  • Time-consuming manual data gathering and analysis
  • Inconsistent quality of recommendations across teams
  • Difficulty scaling expertise across multiple engagements
  • Limited ability to provide real-time insights to clients
  • High costs due to manual labor-intensive processes

Solution: An AI-powered platform that augments consultant capabilities and enables smarter, faster consulting delivery.

Core Platform Features

1. AI Research Assistant

Automatically gather and synthesize industry data, market trends, and competitive intelligence

2. Smart Analysis Engine

AI-powered analysis of client data to identify patterns, opportunities, and risks

3. Automated Report Generation

Create professional reports with AI-generated insights and recommendations

4. Client Dashboard

Real-time visibility into consulting progress and insights for client stakeholders

5. Collaborative Workspace

Tools for consultants to collaborate with colleagues and refine AI-generated insights

Key Use Cases

Market Analysis

AI gathers competitive intelligence and market data, then synthesizes into actionable recommendations for strategic planning

Operational Assessment

Automated analysis of client operations to identify efficiency improvements and cost reduction opportunities

Risk Assessment

AI-powered evaluation of business risks with proactive recommendations and mitigation strategies

Transformation Planning

Intelligent insights to guide digital transformation and change management initiatives

User Experience Design

Consultant Interface

  • Intuitive workflow for creating and managing engagements
  • AI suggestions prominently featured but always human-reviewed
  • Easy customization of AI outputs for client-specific contexts
  • Collaborative tools for team input and refinement

Client Interface

  • Clear visualization of consulting progress
  • Real-time access to insights and recommendations
  • Transparency into methodology and analysis
  • Easy export of reports and findings

Design Principles

Human-Centered AI

AI augments consultant expertise, never replaces it. Every AI suggestion requires human judgment and approval.

Transparency

Clear visibility into how AI generates recommendations and what data sources were used.

Efficiency

Automate repetitive tasks so consultants can focus on higher-value strategic work.

Quality

Ensure AI outputs meet consulting standards with built-in quality review processes.

Potential Impact

For Consultants:

✓ 40-50% reduction in research and analysis time

✓ Improved consistency and quality of recommendations

✓ Ability to take on more engagements

For Clients:

✓ Faster time to insight

✓ Lower consulting costs

✓ More detailed and data-driven recommendations

✓ Better real-time visibility into engagements

Outcome

A forward-thinking platform concept that reimagines enterprise consulting

Combining human expertise with AI capabilities for superior consulting delivery
